Hormang is a **service and product marketplace platform** that connects local buyers with service providers and small‑scale sellers in a single app. It covers both **goods** (products) and **services** (like home cleaning, repairs, nannies, cooking, and other everyday tasks), allowing users to post requests, browse providers, chat, book, pay, and review in one flow.  

Key aspects that are not yet included in the list above but are important for a complete project description:

- **Core idea**:  
  Hormang solves the problem of finding trusted, affordable local services quickly, especially when someone is new to a city or needs a non‑corporate, “human‑scale” provider. It focuses on **local, informal‑to‑semi‑formal micro‑businesses** and individual experts.

- **Target users**:  
  - **Buyers**: residents who need fast, reliable services or local products (e.g., moving, home help, last‑minute help with guests, repairs, food, etc.).  
  - **Providers**: freelancers, artisans, small business owners, homemakers, and others who want an easy way to showcase their work and get clients without big marketing investments.

- **Main product flows (user journeys)**:  
  - Buyer: register → browse/search → send request or choose a provider → chat → book/order → pay (card or cash) → rate.  
  - Provider: register → publish services/products → receive requests/leads → bid or accept → chat → complete job → get paid → earn reputation.  
  - Admin: monitor, moderate, and manage users, orders, payments, and monetization rules.

- **Market context and differentiation**:  
  Hormang competes conceptually with platforms like Ustabor.uz, Naimi.kz, and Soomgo, but focuses on being **simple, mobile‑first, and close to local communities** in Uzbekistan and similar markets. It combines a classic marketplace with **AI‑assisted search** (“smart matching”) to help users describe their needs in plain text and get relevant providers automatically.

- **Business and operating model**:  
  - Revenue is mainly **B2P (business‑to‑provider)**: providers pay for **leads/credits**, **subscriptions for unlimited leads**, and **promotion** (top‑listing, badges).  
  - Buyers can pay services via **card** (through the platform) or **cash** (directly to the provider), which makes the platform attractive for local cash‑dominant markets.

- **Technology stance**:  
  Hormang is built as a **mobile‑first platform** (web + Android/iOS or cross‑platform like Flutter), with a **REST/WS‑based backend**, role‑based auth, and an **AI support layer** for search and recommendations.
